Welcome to Albania!

You're probably wondering, "Why go to Albania? What can I find, and do over there?" Well my friend, I can show you plenty of reasons. So grab your sunscreen, shades, and that trusty camera (that has never failed you yet) for the ride!

Located in Southeastern Europe, Albania is an independant state on the Balkan Peninsula, where it is surrounded by many other states. In the northwest, there is Montenegro, Kosovo in the northeast, east is Macedonia, and finally you have Greece to both south and southeast. Quite a busy neighborhood isn't it? Well we're not paying attention to those other countries. We're geared into see what Albania has to offer as the new adventure!
